About Me

With over 6 years of experience in data engineering, I specialize in building robust, scalable data pipelines that transform raw data into actionable insights. My journey began with a curiosity about how data flows through systems, and it has evolved into a passion for creating efficient, automated solutions that drive business value.

My Data Engineering Journey

My path into data engineering started unexpectedly. While working as a software developer, I was tasked with building a simple ETL process to migrate customer data. What seemed like a routine task opened my eyes to the fascinating world of data transformation, quality, and pipeline orchestration.

The complexity and elegance of turning chaotic, distributed data into clean, reliable information streams captivated me. I dove deep into learning Apache Spark, Kafka, and cloud platforms, eventually transitioning fully into data engineering.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with massive datasets (500TB+ data lakes), real-time streams (1M+ events/minute), and complex ML pipelines. Each project taught me something new about the art and science of data engineering.
