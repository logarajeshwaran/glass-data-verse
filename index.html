About Me

I'm a results-driven Data Engineer with over 6 years of experience building scalable, reliable data pipelines that turn complex, raw data into meaningful insights. My journey started with a curiosity about how data moves—and today, I design robust workflows that power analytics, reporting, and AI solutions across cloud platforms.

My Data Engineering Journey

My path into data engineering was anything but linear. It began in 2017, when—while working as a software developer—I was asked to build a simple ETL job to migrate customer data. That one task sparked a deep fascination with how data flows, transforms, and drives impact across businesses.

Since then, I’ve embraced the world of data engineering—building robust, cloud-native pipelines that handle 500TB+ data lakes, real-time streams with 1M+ events per minute, and ML pipelines that serve mission-critical use cases. From SAS and Teradata migrations to BigQuery optimizations and alerting frameworks, every project has deepened my belief: data quality is not a checkbox—it’s a mindset.

I thrive on automation, performance tuning, and system reliability. But beyond the code, I actively mentor aspiring data engineers, write technical blogs to share my learning on Hashnode, and continuously explore emerging trends in AI, ML, and MLOps.
